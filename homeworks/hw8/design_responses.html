    Dark patterns are deliberate designs used to psychologically influence users without keeping their interest in mind. Examples include Bait and Switch, Disguised Ads, Forced Continuity, Friend Spam, Hidden Costs, Misdirection, Price Comparison Prevention, Privacy Zuckering, Roach Motel, Sneak Into Basket, and Trick Questions. Dark patterns have been around for a while and were once obvious and annoying. However, now with the the increasing stealth of such tricks and algorithms, the only solution we are left with as UI/UX designers is to be ethical with the influence our designs have on users.
